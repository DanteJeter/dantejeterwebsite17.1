Welcome to 104 Paul Avenue

Comfortable living. Added flexibility. Exceptional opportunity.

This versatile Weirton property combines a move-in-ready main residence with additional structures that expand how the property may be used.

Everyday Living

A well-kept brick-front home designed for comfort.

The main residence features three bedrooms and one and a half bathrooms, with functional living spaces designed for everyday comfort. The interior offers inviting rooms, natural light, and a layout that supports both quiet daily living and time spent with family or guests.

Outdoor Living

A full-width rear deck made for relaxing and entertaining.

Step outside to a substantial rear deck that creates an excellent setting for outdoor dining, gatherings, or simply enjoying a quiet evening at home.

Garage & Storage

Room for vehicles, projects, hobbies, and future needs.

The detached two-car garage offers valuable space for parking, storage, workshop use, or hobbies. An additional outbuilding provides even more flexibility for the next owner.

Additional Dwelling

A separately metered rear dwelling adds rare versatility.

The rear accessory dwelling unit may offer possibilities for rental use, guest accommodations, or an extended living arrangement. Buyers should independently verify permitted uses, zoning, occupancy requirements, and rental potential with the appropriate authorities.

Important Updates

Newer roofs and updated mechanical systems.

Both homes feature newer roofs and updated mechanical systems, offering meaningful improvements and added peace of mind for the next owner.

Weirton Location

Convenient access to daily necessities and major routes.

The property is positioned near local amenities, shopping, dining, and major travel routes—bringing together convenience, flexibility, and multiple possible uses in one distinctive offering.

Whether your goals include comfortable residential living, space for an extended household, additional storage, workshop use, or potential supplemental income, 104 Paul Avenue presents an opportunity that is increasingly difficult to find.
