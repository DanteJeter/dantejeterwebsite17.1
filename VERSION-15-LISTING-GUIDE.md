# Version 15 Listing Guide

Each record in `listings-config.js` supports:
- `status`: Active, Coming Soon, Price Improvement, Under Contract, Pending, or Sold
- `featured`: true/false
- `featuredOrder`: 1, 2, or 3 for homepage priority
- verified address, price, facts, MLS, image, and page

Under Contract remains in current listings, receives a gold image ribbon, and keeps its property page live. Sold listings move automatically to Recently Sold.
