# Master Listing Launch Checklist

## Intake and verification
- [ ] Verify address, price, MLS ID, status, beds, baths, square footage, taxes, utilities, and disclosures.
- [ ] Confirm brokerage, license, MLS, fair-housing, advertising, and property-specific requirements.
- [ ] Confirm media rights and seller approval.

## Gallery standard
- [ ] Exterior → Living → Kitchen → Dining → Bedroom 1 → Bedroom 2 → Bedroom 3 → Bathrooms → Deck → Garage → Rear dwelling → Yard.
- [ ] Optimize images, meaningful alt text, hero preload, and lazy-load non-hero images.

## Page and marketing
- [ ] Verify hero, facts, narrative, features, location, financing disclaimer, agent details, and calls to action.
- [ ] Add title, description, canonical URL, social cards, structured data, and campaign/QR URLs.

## Lead system
- [ ] Test every form on desktop and mobile.
- [ ] Confirm required fields and branded thank-you redirect.
- [ ] Confirm new Google Sheets row and email notification.
- [ ] Confirm source, campaign, UTM, URL, referrer, device, and browser fields.
- [ ] Confirm BoldTrail sync after secure server-side integration is enabled.

## Quality assurance
- [ ] Keyboard navigation, labels, contrast, alt text, and focus states.
- [ ] Chrome, Safari, Edge, iPhone, and Android checks.
- [ ] Broken links, image paths, telephone/email links, and final proofreading.
- [ ] Performance review and launch approval.
- [ ] Update CHANGELOG and carry improvements into the master template.
